Law firms sit on mountains of sensitive PDFs, emails, and filings. Finding, summarizing, and reusing that knowledge is slow and expensive. There has been no way around this problem… until now.

Large language models (LLMs) solve this problem extremely well. LLMs for legal work are what industrial machines were for manufacturing: turning slow, manual labor into fast, scalable output. LLMs are the perfect tool for legal work because they excel at parsing long, unstructured text and reasoning across it to surface precedent, risk, and next steps in minutes.

We build and train models specifically tailored for your firm.
