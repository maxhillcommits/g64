Private AI infrastructure for law firms

We build fully private, specialized LLM systems for law firms. Drop documents in; the system ingests, organizes, and enables instant Q&A or summaries—no technical skills required.

Firms sit on mountains of sensitive PDFs, emails, and filings. Searching and reusing that knowledge is slow and expensive. LLMs excel at parsing long, unstructured text and reasoning across it to surface precedent, risk, and next steps in minutes.

General tools (e.g., ChatGPT) fall short: they’re too generic for firm-specific language and workflows, and they aren’t private—sending client data outside the enclave threatens privilege, ethics, and compliance.

The answer: private, fine-tuned LLMs deployed on-prem or VPC, trained on your matters, clause libraries, and templates, with secure retrieval over DMS/eDiscovery, role-based access, encryption, audit logs, and per-matter data residency. Result: review and drafting time cut from days to minutes, higher accuracy via domain tuning + retrieval, and IP stays in-house while the system improves with attorney feedback.

Think of it as a tireless legal assistant that never forgets—speed without sacrificing confidentiality, all inside your environment.
